About Evergreen Yoga Center

Yoga Club supports offering every BODY a variety of affordable yoga practices! As such, most classes listed on Yoga Club's site are low cost or donation based. As yoga is about healthy living, relaxation & meditation, many hold classes in the most natural setting...the great outdoors!

Holistic Yoga Therapy Institute offers one of the regions most unique, flexible, and affordable yoga teacher training programs. Our program is on-going so you can start anytime, select the immersions of interest, and pay as you go! We offer a 200 Hour & 500 Hour Yoga Alliance registered program. Also a 500 Hour & 800 Hour Therapeutic Yoga training program. We are a member school with IAYT, International Association of Yoga Therapists. Trainings offered in Charlotte NC, Raleigh NC, Atlanta GA, Chattanooga TN, Richmond VA.

The benefits of Yoga have been understood and enjoyed for thousands of years by cultures around the world. We created this site to offer a comfortable, easy to understand environment for people who are just beginning to learn about Yoga. Yoga can be a healthy part of anyone’s life – regardless of age, income or location. We hope that the information and resources will be useful as you continue your development and exploration.
